About Us

Aminsson is a dynamic and forwardthinking company dedicated to providing scalable customer experience (CX) and IT solutions tailored to meet the evolving needs of modern businesses.

We Are Aminsson

Aminsson Limited is a software company specializing in Learning Management Systems (LMS), Customer Relationship Management (CRM), and Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) solutions. As the exclusive Blackboard channel partner in Bangladesh, Aminsson offers advanced educational technologies to institutions across the country.

At Aminsson, We have a group of passionate professionals committed to delivering exceptional results across all areas of customer service, technical support, sales, and back-office operations. We believe in building lasting partnerships that bring mutual success.
